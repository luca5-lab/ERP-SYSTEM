ERP System – Business Management & Workflow Platform
Description

Web-based ERP system developed with Django for comprehensive management of a company focused on services and product sales. It enables management of clients, suppliers, quotations, purchase orders, invoicing, work orders (OT), and internal processes by department.

The system centralizes the entire business operation, from quotation to execution and final delivery.

Main Features--------------------

COMMERCIAL MANAGEMENT:
Management of internal services and products (create, edit, delete)
Client and supplier administration
Clear distinction between:
Clients: services/products sold
Suppliers: goods purchased for resale

QUOTATIONS:
Creation of customized quotations
Dynamic price adjustments (e.g., client-specific discounts)
Automatic PDF generation ready to send to clients

PURCHASE ORDERS:
Creation of purchase orders for suppliers
PDF export
Business purchasing management

INVOICING:
Invoice registration
Upload of external PDF invoices (e.g., tax system)
Linking invoices to completed work orders

WORK ORDERS (OT):

Complete workflow management system by department:

Work orders created by administration
Assignment to:
Workshop
Design
Both

DESIGN:
Management of measurements, images, and files
Data editing
Final design approval

WORKSHOP:
Production tracking (printing, cutting, etc.)
Process checklists
Technical data (risks, installation, permissions, etc.)

WORKFLOW

Administration - Design - Workshop - Administration - Invoicing - Completion

USER ROLE MANAGEMENT:
Administrator: full system control and process monitoring

Designer: access to design tasks, editing and approval

Workshop: production execution and task tracking

MONITORING & CONTROL:
Full user activity log (audit system)
Progress tracking by percentage
Administrative dashboard

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT:
Internal stock control
Material input/output tracking
Automatic email alerts for low stock (e.g., 30%)

REPORTS & ANALYTICS:
Sales (paid invoices)
Purchases
Excel export for:
Clients
Suppliers
Sales
Purchases

TECHNOLOGIES USED:
Python
Django
SQLite / MySQL
HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
PDF generation
Automated email notifications

TECHNICAL FEATURES:
Django-based architecture (MVT)
Multi-user system with roles and permissions
Automated business workflow
File handling (PDFs, images)
Modular and scalable system design

PROJECT OBJETIVE:
To develop a complete business management solution that digitalizes commercial, operational, and administrative processes within a single platform.
